My Top-5 Favorite Movies

  1. The Shawshank Redemption
    • Year: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman
    • Brief Description: Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency.
  2. The Godfather
    • Year: 1972
    • Main Actors: Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, James Caan
    • Brief Description: The aging patriarch of an organized crime dynasty transfers control of his clandestine empire to his reluctant son.
  3. The Dark Knight
    • Year: 2008
    • Main Actors: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart
    • Brief Description: When the Joker wreaks havoc on Gotham City, Batman must use all his skills to stop him and restore order to the city.
  4. 12 Angry Men
    • Year: 1957
    • Main Actors: Henry Fonda, Martin Balsam, E.G. Marshall
    • Brief Description: A jury of 12 men must decide the fate of a young man accused of murder, with one dissenting voice pushing for a thorough examination of the evidence.
  5. Schindler's List
    • Year: 1993
    • Main Actors: Liam Neeson, Ben Kingsley, Ralph Fiennes
    • Brief Description: In Poland during World War II, Oskar Schindler gradually becomes concerned for his Jewish workforce after witnessing their persecution by the Nazis.
